About me

Since I was a child I really liked to devise and build simple things, this led me to what I am today. I am passionate about technological solutions and robotics so that they help people in their daily lives.

My mentality at work is to be persevering, creative and to learn something new every day. I am currently very attracted to working on robotics projects or devising and implementing mechatronic solutions. I like to work in an environment where creativity and innovation are fostered.

I am proud to have been a finalist in the ICSR-2022 social robotics competition, also to have won in the PIIB 2021 biomedical engineering program, and to have been part of the team that won the LINKING 2023 hackathon to promote civic education in a district of Lima.

In the future I aspire to implement artificial intelligence in robots so that they can perform tasks autonomously and safely. I want to leave as a legacy solutions that allow people to have a better quality of life.
